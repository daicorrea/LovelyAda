Who was Ada Lovelace?

  • Ada Lovelace's real name was Augusta Ada King-Noel. She was the Countess of Lovelace and that's why she turned into "Ada Lovelace".
  • She was born in 10 December 1815
  • Her father was the famous poet George, Lord Byron. However, when Ada was one month old, her father left Ada and her mother. He never saw her again.
  • Ada's mom, Anne Isabella Milbanke ("Annabella"), loved math and wanted to prevent Ada to follow her father's steps with poetry. So she hired a lot of tutors with Math and science sckills to promote Ada's interest.
  • Ada was still interested in her father and was asked to be buried beside him.
